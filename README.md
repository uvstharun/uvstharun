## 🧠 About Me

I'm a **Data Scientist** specializing in **healthcare analytics and predictive modeling**, currently building AI-driven forecasting systems for **LA County Department of Health Services** — one of the largest public health systems in the United States.

My work sits at the intersection of **machine learning**, **clinical operations**, and **public health equity**. I'm not just building models — I'm building tools that help allocate hospital beds, predict patient surges, and reduce operational blind spots for care teams serving hundreds of thousands of patients.

- 🏥 **Domain:** Public Health · Hospital Operations · Radiology Analytics · ICU Capacity Planning  
- 🔬 **Current Project:** SARIMAX-based ICU bed utilization forecasting with demographic & clinical exogenous variables  
- 📊 **Stack:** Python · SQL (Vertica) · Tableau · scikit-learn · statsmodels · pandas  
- 🎯 **Direction:** Applying LLMs and agentic AI workflows to healthcare operations  
- 📍 **Based in:** Los Angeles, CA

## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🏥 [ICU Bed Utilization Forecasting — LA General Hospital](https://github.com/uvstharun)
> **SARIMAX · Python · Healthcare Operations**

Multi-phase forecasting system for ICU capacity at LA General, incorporating patient demographics (age, gender) and clinical condition codes (CCSR) as exogenous variables. Automated AIC/BIC/RMSE/MAPE grid search with residual diagnostics. Currently in Phase 3 (model governance & production monitoring).

`Python` `statsmodels` `SARIMAX` `SQL/Vertica` `Tableau`

---

### 🧠 [Brain Tumor Detection via CNN](https://github.com/uvstharun/Brain_Tumor_Detection_CNN)
> **Deep Learning · Medical Imaging · TensorFlow/Keras**

Convolutional neural network trained on MRI scans to classify brain tumors with **>90% accuracy**. Includes full preprocessing pipeline (normalization, 80/20 train-test split), architecture design (Conv → Pooling → Dense → Softmax), and evaluation via confusion matrix, precision, recall, and F1-score.

`TensorFlow` `Keras` `CNN` `Medical Imaging` `Python`

---

### ✈️ [Airline Review Sentiment Analysis](https://github.com/uvstharun/Airline_Review_Sentiment_Analysis)
> **NLP · VADER · TextBlob · Tableau**

End-to-end NLP pipeline analyzing 200,000+ airline reviews using VADER and TextBlob sentiment scoring. Surfaced service-level drivers of passenger dissatisfaction across domestic and international carriers. Visualized trends in Tableau and identified COVID-period sentiment shifts.

`Python` `NLTK` `VADER` `TextBlob` `Tableau` `NLP`

---

### 💳 [Credit Card Default Prediction](https://github.com/uvstharun/Credit_Card_Default)
> **Classification · R · Risk Modeling**

Statistical modeling project predicting credit card default risk using classification algorithms in R. Covers EDA, feature selection, and model evaluation with accuracy and ROC metrics.

`R` `Classification` `Logistic Regression` `Risk Modeling`
